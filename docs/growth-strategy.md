# Voodoo Momo — Instagram Growth & Conversion Playbook

Goal: turn Instagram from a poster board into a **footfall engine** — more people
discovering us, and more of them actually walking into the Wagholi shop.

This is a strategy the human applies. Where the agent already helps, it's flagged
**[agent]**; where it needs you (offers, Stories, Google Business), it's flagged
**[manual]**.

---

## 1. The funnel we're optimising

```
Discover (Reel/hashtag/geotag)  →  Profile visit  →  Save / Share / Follow
    →  Intent (offer, hours, directions)  →  VISIT  →  Repeat + tell a friend
```

Every post should move someone one step down. A pretty momo photo that gives no
reason or way to act is a dead end. The single biggest lever for a local food shop
is **occasion + offer + a clear next action**, not more reach.

## 2. Who we convert (and their trigger)

| Audience | Where | Trigger that makes them visit |
|---|---|---|
| **JSPM college students** | 5-min walk | Cheap, fast, shareable; between/after classes; group hangs |
| **WFH / working professionals** | Wagholi | Evening unwind, rainy-day comfort, quick dinner |
| **Families** | Nyati Commercial area | Weekend treat, variety, sharing platters |

The students are the flywheel — they post, tag, and bring friends. Bias content and
offers toward them without alienating families.

## 3. The five conversion levers

1. **Occasion framing** — tie momos to a moment: post-college craving, rainy evening,
   Friday group hang, exam-week fuel, weekend family platter. **[agent]** — feed the
   moment into the brief `notes`.
2. **A real offer** — see the offer menu (§6). Offers convert; "we exist" posts don't.
3. **A clear action CTA** — geotag + one of: *"Show this post for…"*, *"Get directions
   (link in bio)"*, *"DM MOMO to reserve a table"*, *"Happy hours 12–5, walk in."*
   **[agent]** captions can carry these; keep them on-brand (no hype).
4. **Local discovery** — **geotag every post** (Voodoo Momo / Wagholi / Nyati
   Commercial), Wagholi+Pune hashtags, and a complete **Google Business Profile**
   with menu, hours, photos (this is where "momos near me" searches land). **[manual]**
5. **Social proof** — repost customer photos/Stories, show the queue/steamer, pin
   reviews to a Highlight. Nothing sells food like other people eating it. **[manual]**

## 4. Content mix (tuned for conversion)

Shift from "mostly product shots" to a mix that drives action. Weekly target:

| Type | /week | Purpose | Format |
|---|---|---|---|
| Product spotlight (a specific variety + occasion) | 2 | Craving trigger | Feed/Reel |
| **Offer / event** (happy hours, combo, student deal) | 1–2 | **Direct conversion** | Feed + Story |
| Community / UGC repost (a regular, a review) | 1 | Trust + reach | Feed/Story |
| Behind-the-scenes (steamer, fresh folding, prep) | 1 | Authenticity | Reel/Story |
| **Stories — daily** (offer of the day, poll, countdown) | 7 | Top-of-mind → visit | Story |

Note: the agent's `strategy` node has an `offer_event` pillar set to frequency **0
("as needed")** — **activate it** (≈1–2/week) by putting an offer in the campaign brief.
The agent posts feed/carousel; **Stories, Highlights, and geotags are manual.**

## 5. Formats — what each is for

- **Reels** = discovery (non-followers). Sizzling grill, cheese pull, steam, "which momo
  are you?" — 7–15s, trending-but-tasteful. This is how new students find us.
- **Carousels** = education/decision. "31 varieties, ranked by spice", "first time? start
  here" — high saves = intent.
- **Stories (daily)** = the conversion channel. Offer of the day, "swipe to order",
  polls ("Steam or Fry tonight?"), countdown to happy hours. Replies → DMs → visits.
- **Highlights** (set up once, **[manual]**): `Menu` · `Hours & Location` · `Offers` ·
  `Reviews` · `Happy Hours`. This is your always-on storefront.

## 6. Offer menu (pick, rotate, never run all at once)

Keep margins safe; the offer is a *reason to come now*, not a giveaway.

- **Happy Hours 12–5** — quieter hours, flat combo price. (Already in the brand.)
- **Student combo** — show college ID → momos + drink at a set price. Owns the JSPM crowd.
- **Rainy-day special** — monsoon (Jun–Sep): hot momos + chai combo. On-brand seasonal.
- **Group platter** — "4 friends, 4 varieties" sharing deal. Drives group visits.
- **First-visit** — "New here? Show this post for a free piece." Converts discovery.
- **Loyalty** — buy 5 plates, 6th free (paper card or a code word). Drives repeat.
- **Refer-a-friend** — "Bring a friend, both get 10% off." Turns fans into a channel.

Track redemptions with a **code word per offer** ("say VOODOO12") so you know what works.

## 7. Engagement → conversion tactics

- Reply to **every** comment/DM within the hour (the agent's Engagement loop helps, but
  keep it warm and human). Fast replies = bookings.
- Weekly **poll** in Stories ("Tonight's pick?") — cheap engagement that primes a visit.
- **Repost UGC** the same day, tagged. Ask happy customers to tag @voodoomomo.
- Light **contest** monthly: "Tag 2 friends you'd share momos with" → free plate draw.
  Drives reach + brings a group in to redeem.
- Partner with neighbours **[manual]**: JSPM clubs/fests, nearby gyms, PG hostels,
  cloud-kitchen collabs — cross-post and co-offer.

## 8. Cadence & timing

- **Feed:** 4–5×/week (the agent's weekly calendar covers this).
- **Stories:** daily, and always around **11:30am (pre-lunch)** and **6–8pm (evening
  rush)** when the decision to eat is being made.
- **Reels:** 2×/week — your discovery engine; don't skip them.
- Post product/offer content **before** the craving window, not after.

## 9. Measure what matters (weekly)

Vanity = likes/followers. **Conversion signals** = the ones below. Check IG Insights
+ Google Business weekly:

- **Profile visits** and **"Get Directions" / call taps** (intent).
- **Saves & shares** per post (saves = "I'll come try this").
- **Story replies & poll votes** (warm leads).
- **Reach from non-followers** on Reels (discovery working?).
- **Offer redemptions** by code word (the real ROI number).

> The agent's `analytics` node is currently a stub — metric writeback isn't automated
> yet, so pull these from IG Insights manually for now. (Automating this is a tracked
> next step.)

Each week: keep the top-2 performing post types, cut the bottom one, rotate the offer.

## 10. First 30 days (quick start)

1. **Week 1 — foundation [manual]:** complete Google Business Profile (menu, hours,
   photos, "order/directions"); set up the 5 Highlights; add a Linktree/bio link with
   Directions + Menu + WhatsApp.
2. **Week 1 — switch on offers:** launch **Happy Hours 12–5** + **Student combo** with
   code words. Put them in the agent brief so captions carry the CTA.
3. **Week 2 — discovery:** ship 2 Reels; start daily Stories with a poll.
4. **Week 3 — proof:** begin reposting UGC; ask 5 regulars to tag you.
5. **Week 4 — referral:** launch refer-a-friend + one tag-2-friends contest.
6. **Every week:** review §9 metrics, rotate the offer, double down on the winner.

## 11. Stay on-brand while converting

Conversion does **not** mean hype. Keep the brand voice:

- ✅ "Rainy evening in Wagholi? Happy hours till 5 — walk in. 🥟"
- ❌ "🔥🔥 BEST MOMOS IN PUNE!! DM NOW!!!"

Soft-but-clear beats loud. A single offer, one emoji, a geotag, and a real next step
will out-convert a wall of exclamation marks every time.
